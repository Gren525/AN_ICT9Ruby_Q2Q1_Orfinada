Dogecoin

Dogecoin (DOGE) is an open-source, peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that was made as a parody of the crypto market following the establishment of Bitcoin. It was originally created as a light-hearted joke for crypto enthusiasts, but it quickly exploded to be one of the leading cryptocurrencies in use today.

Value: 23.23 PHP / 0.39 USD

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain platform that establishes a peer-to-peer network that securely executes and verifies application code, called smart contracts. Smart contracts allow participants to transact with each other without a trusted central authority.

Value: 197,518.14 PHP / 3,351.46 USD

Solana

Solana is an open infrastructure for building scalable crypto apps. The architecture is censorship-resistant, fast, and secure, and designed to facilitate global adoption. To keep time on the blockchain, Solana employs an innovative process known as Proof of History.

Value: 15,328.59 PHP / 260.08 USD
